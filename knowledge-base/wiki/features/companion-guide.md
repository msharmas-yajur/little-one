# Companion Guide

The full 12-month guide for the grown-up, in two forms:
- **On-site:** `companion.html` → `/companion/`, auto-rendered from
  `_data/arc.yml` so it never drifts from the cards.
- **Document:** a Word `.docx` generated separately (from the same arc data) for
  printing/sharing. Not part of the Jekyll build; regenerate when the arc changes.

Includes the framing (gardener not carpenter), how-to-use, all 12 months, a
closing note, and a care disclaimer (general guidance, not personalized advice;
confirm child-specific questions with a pediatrician).

**Acceptance:** `/companion/` lists all 12 months with stance + invitations; the
docx matches.
