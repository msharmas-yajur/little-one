## What it is
- **Video:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NHi3ataTH34 — a demo of the board book
  *Where's Mrs Cat?* being read/played with (turning pages, lifting flaps).
- **Book:** *Where's Mrs Cat?*, **Felt Flaps** series, illustrated by Ingela P.
  Arrhenius, publisher Nosy Crow (2022). Same illustrator as the bestselling
  *Peekaboo* series. (Already noted in `raw/research/famous-books.md`.)

## The mechanic (what to recreate, in our own art)
- **Predictable peek-a-boo pattern:** each spread asks "**Where's [animal]?**",
  a big toddler-proof **flap** hides someone, you **lift the flap** → "**There
  she is!**" Reveal + name. Repeats across a few animals.
- **Sturdy, big flaps**; a satisfying **turn of the page** between spreads.
- **Mirror finale** on the last flap (the child sees themselves).
- Why it works for ~1yo: **object permanence** (gone → back), anticipation and
  repetition, naming, and the delight of causing the reveal.

## Copyright caution (hard rule)
Recreate the **mechanic and feel**, never the book's art or characters. Our flaps,
animals, and the "surprise" are **original SVG** (see `design-principles.md`).
